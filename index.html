Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes! Our basic vocabulary lessons are free for everyone to help spread the joy of learning.

3. Do I need to create an account?

No, you can start learning immediately! Most of our vocabulary lessons and interactive tools are open to everyone. However, creating a free account allows you to save your progress, bookmark difficult words, and track your learning journey over time.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Consistency is key! We recommend exploring one Lesson per day on our platform. Use our Pronunciation tool to hear the words, read the example sentences to understand context, and try to use the new words in your daily conversations.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Currently, we focus on providing free interactive tools for self-study. While we don't offer formal certificates yet, you can track your "Learning Complete" status across different levels.
